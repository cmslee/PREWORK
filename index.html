Hi, I'm Casey. I used to study and write about pop culture in China and Japan. Now, I'm happily a fan who occasionally translates. I chose this image to represent me because it reminds me of visiting East Asia, which I love to do, but is not the most accessible activity right now. I especially like bronze objects that are shaped like animals or, like this one, stand on three legs.
